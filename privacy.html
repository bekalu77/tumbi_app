Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

Welcome to Tumbi Marketplace. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a functional marketplace experience, we collect the following information:

  • Account Data: Name, phone number, and optional email address.
  • Profile Information: Profile pictures or company logos.
  • Listing Content: Titles, descriptions, prices, and images of products or services you post.
  • Chat Messages: Communication between buyers and sellers within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate the buying and selling of construction materials and services.
  • To allow other users to contact you regarding your listings.
  • To verify vendors and maintain a trusted marketplace environment.
  • To improve the functionality and security of the Tumbi app.

3. Data Storage and Protection

Your information is stored securely using industry-standard cloud infrastructure (Neon and Cloudflare). We implement security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information necessary to fulfill the services of the app (e.g., displaying your contact phone number to potential buyers so they can call you).

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your information. You can:

  • Edit your profile details at any time through the "Account Settings" menu.
  • Delete your listings whenever they are no longer available.
  • Contact us if you wish to deactivate your account entirely.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes via the app interface.
